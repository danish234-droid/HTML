DELICIOUS CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

A classic recipe for soft,chewy and irrestible chip cookies

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:

  • ½cup (1 stick) unsalted butter ,softened
  • ½cup granulated sugar
  • ¼packed light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

add-ins:

  • 1 cup chocolate chip (milk,semi-sweet or dark)

Instruction:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F)
  2. In a bowl, cream together 1 cup butter and 1 cup sugar.
  3. Add 2 eggs and 1 tsp vanilla extract; mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk 2½ cups flour, 1 tsp baking soda, and ½ tsp salt.
  5. Gradually mix dry ingredients into wet mixture.
  6. Stir in 1-2 cups of chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop dough onto a baking tray, spacing evenly
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown
  9. Let cool on a rack before serving. Enjoy!
